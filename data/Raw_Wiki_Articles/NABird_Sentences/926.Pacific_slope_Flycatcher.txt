0 the pacific-slope flycatcher (empidonax difficilis) is a small insectivorous bird of the family tyrannidae .
1   it is native to coastal regions of western north america , including the pacific ocean and the southern gulf of california , as far north as british columbia and southern alaska , but is replaced in the inland regions by the cordilleran flycatcher .

9  they also enter swarms of gnats , mosquitos and wherever such insects congregate .
