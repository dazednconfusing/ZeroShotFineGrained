0 the chihuahuan raven (corvus cryptoleucus) is a species of bird in the family corvidae that is native to the united states and mexico .
1   it was formerly known as the american white-necked raven , and has the proportions of a common raven with a heavy bill , but is about the same size as a carrion crow , or slightly larger than the american crow ({convert|44|-|51|cm|abbr=on} long) .
2  the plumage is all-black with a rich purple-blue gloss in good light .
3  like the forest raven , little raven , fan-tailed raven and australian raven , it is one of the smaller raven species .
4  the larger species of raven are the common raven , thick-billed raven , white-necked raven and brown-necked raven , with the common and thick-billed ravens being the world's largest raven species and the little and fan-tailed ravens being the smallest .
5  the chihuahuan raven is very similar in appearance to the australian raven , although with dark brown irises and whiter feather bases .
6  the nasal bristles extend farther down the top of the bill than in any other corvus species to about two-thirds the length .
7  the base of the neck feathers are white-ish (seen only when ruffled in strong wind) .
8  the bill , legs and feet are black .
9 ==distribution and habitat==the chihuahuan raven occurs in the southwestern and midwestern united states and northern mexico , including southeastern arizona , southern new mexico , southeastern colorado , western kansas , western oklahoma , and southern and western texas .
10 ==behavior=====diet===it feeds on cultivated cereal grains , insects and many other invertebrates , small reptiles , carrion and scraps of human food , cactus fruits , eggs and nestlings .
11 ===nesting===the nest is built in either trees , large shrubs or sometimes even in old buildings .
12  there are usually 57 eggs laid relatively late in the year during may so as to take advantage of the insect food for their young in their more arid environment .
13 ===voice===the voice is similar to that of the common raven with "pruk-pruk" sounds and other croaks but is not as deep in tone .
14  like all corvids , the chihuahuan raven is capable of vocal mimicry , however this behavior is mostly recorded in captivity and , although rarely , in the wild .
15 ==taxonomy==a 2005 molecular study reviewed segments of dna of the common raven and found that chihuahuan raven are genetically nested within common ravens based on mitochondrial dna .
16  that is , common ravens from the california clade are more similar in mtdna to chihuahuan ravens than they are to common ravens in the holarctic clade .
