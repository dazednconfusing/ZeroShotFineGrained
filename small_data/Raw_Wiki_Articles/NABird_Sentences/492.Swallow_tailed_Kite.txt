13  nests are built in trees , usually near water .

22 == conservation in the united states ==swallow-tailed kites are not listed as endangered or threatened by the federal government in the united states .
23  they are listed as endangered by the state of south carolina and as threatened by the state of texas .
24  they are listed as "rare" by the state of georgia .
25  destruction of habitats is chiefly responsible for the decline in numbers .
26  a key conservation area is the lower suwannee national wildlife refuge in florida .
