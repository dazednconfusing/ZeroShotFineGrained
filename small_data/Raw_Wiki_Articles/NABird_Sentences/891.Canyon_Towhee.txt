0 the canyon towhee (melozone fusca) is a bird of the family emberizidae .
1 ==taxonomy==the taxonomy of the group of towhees to which this species belongs is debated .
2  at the higher level , some authors place the towhees in the family fringillidae .
3  within the genus , there has been dispute about whether the canyon towhee is a distinct species from the california towhee (melozone crissalis) found in coastal regions from oregon and california in the united states through baja california in mexico .
4   at present , molecular genetics seems to have settled this issue in favour of separation of the species .
5 ==description==it is {convert|19|to|25|cm|in|abbr=on} long , and has a noticeably long tail , at {convert|8.2|to|11|cm|in|abbr=on} .
6  this species weighs from {convert|36.5|to|67|g|oz|abbr=on}, though on average weigh only around {convert|45|g|oz|abbr=on} .
7  among standard measurements , the wing chord is {convert|8.2|to|10.1|cm|in|abbr=on}, the bill is {convert|1.4|to|1.7|cm|in|abbr=on} and the tarsus is {convert|2.3|to|2.7|cm|in|abbr=on} .
8  it is earthy brown in colour , with somewhat lighter underparts and a somewhat darker head with a rufous cap (except that birds in central mexico have the cap the same color as the back); there is also a slightly reddish area beneath the tail .
9  there is little sexual dimorphism .
10 ==distribution and habitat==the towhee is native to lower-lying areas from arizona , southern colorado , and western texas south to northwestern oaxaca , mexico , mostly avoiding the coasts .
11   its natural habitat is brush or chaparral .
12 ==behaviour==the towhee feeds on the ground or in low scrub rather than in the tree canopy .
13   near human habitation , it is often seen in parking lots , where it feeds on insects on the cars' grilles and takes cover under the cars when disturbed .
